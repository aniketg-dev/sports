Data-Driven Performance

Elev8's comprehensive analytics system harnesses AI to provide deep insights into athlete performance. We collect data from wearables, on-field sensors, and tests, then use sophisticated algorithms to identify patterns, predict outcomes, and generate actionable insights for coaches and athletes.

Data Source Insights Benefits
Wearables Heart rate, movement, sleep Optimize training and recovery
On-Field Sensors Speed, acceleration, positioning Improve tactics and performance
Performance Tests Strength, flexibility, skills Track progress and identify areas for improvement
Video Analysis Technique, strategy, opponents Enhance skill and game planning
